Software Developer Games Developer Web Developer
Description The application of a systematic, disciplined, quantifiable approach to the development, operation, and maintenance of software. Creating and designing a game's look and how it plays, animating characters and objects, creating audio, programming, localisation, testing and producing. A web developer tends to the technical aspects of creating websites. He or she build and maintain websites and web applications.
Educational Requirements Have to have a relevant computing qualification or degree, however there are companies that run trainee programmes for those with AS levels. Bachelor's degree preferred Can vary anywhere from a high school degree to a bachelor's degree.However, a bachelor's degree is typically required for advancement to a project manager role or for back-end development positions.
Expected skills Communication; Analytical and Problem Solving; Detail-oriented; Creativity; Knowledge of programming skills is a prerequisite. Programmer, with various specialisms such as network, engine, toolchain and artificial intelligence; designer, including concept artist, animator and 3D modeller;special effects technician. Communication; The ability to manage a complex range of tasks and meet deadlines; The ability to work independently and in mixed teams; A logical approach to problem solving;
